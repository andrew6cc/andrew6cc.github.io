About Us


The program prepares young minds to work and thrive in our increasingly technological world and global economy.


The Jamaica Gateway to the Sciences’ Robotics Team (JGS Robotics Team) exists to encourage the development of students’ interests in the areas of STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics). By doing so, the program prepares young minds to work and thrive in our increasingly technological world and global economy. The team participates in a competition held by FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology), a program developed in 1989 in order to inspire more interest in the fields of science and technology. In this competition, our team designs, programs, and builds a robot that performs an annually assigned task -- all in the span of six weeks. This year, our team, Team 6636, will compete in two different regional competitions in March and April against over sixty teams and takes place at Hofstra University.

Our Origin


FIRST'S MISSION: FIRST was founded with a mission to transform our culture by creating a world where young people dream of becoming science and technology leaders.


Our team started in 2014 as an after school club. Back then, we didn't compete in any official competitions, but we learned a lot about each other and teamwork through various fundraising activities, team-building exercises, and planning. We officially registered for FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) in 2016 and got our team number 6636. In 2017, we competed in the Hudson Valley Regional in Suffern, NY. We learned A LOT during that season. This year, our team, Team 6636, will compete in two different regional competitions in March and April against over sixty teams and takes place at Hofstra University.

WHAT WE DO


ENGINEERING

Our Engineering Department builds, programs, and drives our robot for our competition.

BUSINESS

Our Business Department helps the Engineering Department buy their parts by participating in many fundraising activities and more. They are also in charge of the social media accounts and do web and graphics design.

LEADERSHIP

Our Managers and Directors of the Business and Engineering Department lead the team and make sure everything is going well and our team members are focusing on their tasks.
